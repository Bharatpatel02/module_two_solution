Rohit Sharma


Rohit Gurunath Sharma is an Indian cricketer and the Vice Captain of team India in limited-over cricket. He plays for Mumbai in domestic cricket and captains Mumbai Indians in the Indian Premier League. He is a right-handed top-order batsman and an occasional right-arm off-break bowler.

Virat Kohli


Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and the current captain of the India national team. A right-handed top-order batsman. He plays for Delhi in domestic cricket and for Royal Challengers Bangalore in the Indian Premier League as captain of the franchise since 2013.

M.S.Dhoni


Honorary Lieutenant Colonel Mahendra Singh Dhoni, is a former Indian international cricketer who captained the Indian national team in limited-overs formats from 2007 to 2017 and in Test cricket from 2008 to 2014. MS Dhoni is the only captain in the history of cricket to win all ICC trophies.
